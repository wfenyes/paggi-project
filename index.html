TIM PAGGI

Writer of Office Horror

Tim Paggi is a playwright, poet and writer of office horror. His work uses dark humor and cosmic horror to explore issues of identity and free will in American workplaces. He is the author of the novella, “How to Kill Friends and Eviscerate People,” and the forthcoming “The Other Side of the Hallway” from Akinoga Press in 2025. His poetry chapbook "Workforced" won the 2015 Plork "Play/Work" Award for Creative Writing and Publication Arts.

Currently, he seeks representation for his novel, “Welcome to the Team.” He holds an MFA from the University of Baltimore, and lives with his wife in Johnston Square.
